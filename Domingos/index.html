Domingo's Family Story

Domingo Sr. began making pastries for family and friends in Caracas, Venezuela in the 60’s. However, Domingo Sr. and his family relocated to the US in 1983 for better opportunities for themselves, but especially for their son, Domingo. In 2000, Domingo Sr. was finally able to realize his dream of opening his own bakery/restaurant at our old location in Newark, NJ. In 2015, Domingo took over the business from his father and moved the shop its current location because of its easier accessibility.
